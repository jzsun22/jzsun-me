## Design Context

### Users
Hiring managers, recruiters, and design peers reviewing Jocelyn's portfolio. They're scanning quickly to assess design thinking, process, and craft — then diving deeper into case studies that interest them. The site needs to make a strong first impression and reward deeper reading.

### Brand Personality
**Thoughtful, clean, warm.** Professional but approachable. The design should feel intentional and considered — like the work it showcases. Subtle personal touches (the typed.js hero, the footer easter egg) add warmth without undermining credibility.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual tone**: Calm, airy, confident. Generous whitespace. Nothing loud or trendy.
- **Color palette**: Muted naturals — deep teal (`#284c4c`), dusty rose (`#a45e5f`), blue-gray (`#749eaf`), warm grays for text. White background.
- **Typography**: Instrument Sans (body/UI), Instrument Serif (editorial accents — mobile hero, bylines), Inconsolata (code/mono/structured meta). Same foundry pairing for coherence; serif used sparingly for warmth, not structure.
- **Theme**: Light mode only.
- **Anti-references**: Do NOT replicate the current Webflow site at jzsun.me. The new build should evolve beyond it — feel more refined and distinctive.
- **No strong external references** — direction should emerge from the personality and existing design tokens.

### Design Principles
1. **Content-first**: The work is the hero. Design should frame and elevate case studies, not compete with them.
2. **Quiet confidence**: Let quality speak through restraint — whitespace, subtle transitions, considered typography. No flashiness.
3. **Warmth through details**: Small, intentional touches (hover states, micro-interactions, personal copy) create connection without clutter.
4. **Responsive by design**: Layouts should feel native to each device, not just squeezed to fit. Tablet and phone are first-class experiences.
5. **Accessibility as baseline**: Semantic HTML, keyboard navigation, sufficient contrast, and meaningful aria attributes are non-negotiable.
